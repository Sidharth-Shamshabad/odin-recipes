Eggplant Parmesan

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Dip eggplant slices in beaten egg, then in bread crumbs.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until golden brown.
